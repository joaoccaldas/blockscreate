How to Play

  • Move: A/D or ← →  ·  Jump: W / ↑ / Space
  • Mine: hold left-click on a nearby block
  • Place / Eat: right-click with a block or food selected
  • Hotbar: keys 1–9 or scroll wheel
  • Inventory: E  ·  Crafting: C
  • Creative flight: press F, then W/S to rise and fall
  • Goal (Survival): gather & build to earn ✨ Civ Points and open the portal to the next era.
