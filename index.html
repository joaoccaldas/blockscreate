How to Play

🖥️ Desktop

  • Move: A/D or ← →
  • Jump: W / ↑ / Space
  • Mine: hold left-click
  • Place / Eat: right-click
  • Hotbar: 1–9 or scroll
  • Inventory: E   Craft: C
  • Build/Mine toggle: Q
  • Fly (creative): F
  • Pause / Menu: Esc or P

📱 Mobile

  • Move: ◀ ▶ buttons (bottom-left)
  • Jump: ⤴ button
  • Mine / Build: tap the world
  • ⛏ / 🧱 button: switch mine ↔ build
  • Hotbar: tap a slot to select
  • Menu: ☰ top-right
🦖 Age of Dinosaurs: the first era drops you among living dinosaurs — graze-friendly stegosaurs and triceratops, plus deadly raptors and a T-Rex. Tame fire, build shelter, and survive ☄️ meteor showers that build toward an asteroid impact. Cold nights make shelter, torches, and campfires matter before dawn.
🎯 Goal (Survival): finish the objectives and earn ✨ Civ Points to open the next era. Explore expanding worlds, uncover physical clues, and branch alternate histories. Unlocked eras also become free-build Creative worlds.
