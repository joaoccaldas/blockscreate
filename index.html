How to Play

🖥️ Desktop

  • Move: A/D or ← →
  • Jump: W / ↑ / Space
  • Mine: hold left-click
  • Place / Eat: right-click
  • Hotbar: 1–9 or scroll
  • Inventory: E   Craft: C
  • Build/Mine toggle: Q
  • Fly (creative): F
  • Pause / Menu: Esc or P

📱 Mobile

  • Move: ◀ ▶ buttons (bottom-left)
  • Jump: ⤴ button
  • Mine / Build: tap the world
  • ⛏ / 🧱 button: switch mine ↔ build
  • Hotbar: tap a slot to select
  • Menu: ☰ top-right
✦ Before Life: bring primordial ingredients together and cause the first spark of life.
🫧 First Cell: continue as a tiny proto-life form near warm vents. Absorb nutrients, gather minerals, form a membrane, and stabilize a proto-cell before evolving into the 🦖 Age of Dinosaurs.
🎯 Goal (Survival): finish the objectives and earn ✨ Civ Points to open the next era. Explore expanding worlds, uncover physical clues, and branch alternate histories. Unlocked eras also become free-build Creative worlds.
🛡️ Iron Age tip: raiders march on your town and smash through weak walls — build tall stone ramparts and gates to physically hold them off.

⚙️ Settings
